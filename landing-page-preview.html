Anonymous product feedback

Tell us what worked well and what did not.

This feedback form is meant to show up in three places: on the website, on the teaser campaign page, and right after login inside the app. It can stay anonymous, and it should help us capture both wins and friction while the experience is still fresh.

What worked well? Ask users what felt useful, clear, or valuable so we know what to protect as the product grows.
What did not work? Ask what felt confusing, broken, slow, or missing so the team can spot the highest-friction issues quickly.
What should we improve next? Capture one recommendation in the user's own words so the feedback loop turns into product priorities instead of just raw comments.

Why It Exists

Big cities make discovery easy and belonging hard. Connect My Tribe helps users find communities that fit, not just activities to scroll past.

Why It Feels Different

Meetup can feel clunky, Eventbrite is transactional, and Facebook is noisy. Connect My Tribe is built around intentional, repeat, in-person connection.

Who It Is For

People in Chicago who want to make connections, discover events that fit their interests, and build community in a more personal way.
